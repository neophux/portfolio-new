About Me

I am the most passionate designer you'll ever work with. I am currently working as a software engineer in one of the best organisation in India. But as the fate would have it, I am more inclined to designing the products from scratch rather than developing it.

So to pursue my passion for design, I started taking up small to medium sized projects to gain practical knowledge of UI//UX design and to understand how stakeholder communications, user research, storyboarding and usability testing works in the real world. My arsenal consists of Visual Design, Motion Design and Interaction Design.

Currently I am actively looking forward to test the waters of design industry and help the organisations grow along with me.
